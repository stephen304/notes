## Caveats of validity
###
* If the premise is always false or the conclusion is always true, the argument is valid.

### Syntactic Validity

* Evaluate the form

## Argument forms
* Syllogism: p -> q && q -> r ... p -> r
* Modus Ponens: p-> q && p ... q
* Modus Tollens: p -> q && !q ... !p
